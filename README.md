# 🏬 Dubai Mall — Interactive Sales Deck

A cinematic, browser-based **interactive sales deck** designed to position Dubai Mall as a high-performance global destination for **retail leasing, brand partnerships, and large-scale events**.

Built as a **presentation tool (not a traditional website)**, this experience enables stakeholders to explore the property non-linearly while immediately understanding its **scale, energy, and commercial value**.

# 🎯 Project Objective

Traditional sales processes for large retail destinations are fragmented — involving videos, PDFs, spreadsheets, and verbal explanations.

This project replaces that with a **single, cohesive interactive system** that:

* Tells the story without explanation
* Creates emotional buy-in within seconds
* Demonstrates commercial value clearly
* Supports both **live presentations** and **self-guided exploration**

---

# 🧠 Product Thinking Approach

Instead of building a static website, this was approached as a:

> **Narrative-controlled sales system**

### Key Principles:

* **Non-linear navigation** → users can jump between sections instantly
* **Video-first storytelling** → emotion before information
* **Layered persuasion** → emotion → logic → validation → action
* **Minimal UI, maximum impact**

---

# 🧩 Experience Structure

The deck is structured like a modern Digideck-style presentation:

## 1. Hero (Entry Layer)

* Full-screen cinematic video
* Immediate emotional hook
* Sets tone: scale, energy, ambition

---

## 2. Overview (Impact Layer)

* High-impact headline
* Key metrics (footfall, outlets, positioning)
* Establishes global dominance instantly

---

## 3. Retail Opportunities (Business Layer)

* Claim → Proof → Comparison model
* Performance-driven messaging
* Benchmark comparison table
* Focus: **Why brands perform better here**

---

## 4. Event Platform (Experience Layer)

* Cinematic event visuals
* Emphasis on scale and visibility
* Positions the mall as a **global activation stage**

---

## 5. Audience & Reach (Validation Layer)

* 3D-inspired global visualization
* International audience + purchasing power
* Demonstrates **quality of demand**, not just quantity

---

## 6. Final CTA (Conversion Layer)

* Minimal, focused design
* Aurora-style animated background
* Clear action: **Start Partnership**
